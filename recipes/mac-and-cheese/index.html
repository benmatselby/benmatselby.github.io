Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 300g Macaroni
  • 900ml milk
  • 75g grated Parmesan
  • 75g grated mature Cheddar
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: Bread crumbs

Method

  • Cook the Macaroni.
  • Warm the milk - not boiling.
  • Melt the better in a pan and then add the flour into a paste.
  • Very slowly add the milk, whisking all the time.
  • When all the milk is mixed in, add the grated cheese, parmesan, and dijon into the mix.
  • Season to taste
  • When the pasta is ready, mix in the sauce
  • But all the mix into an oven proof dish
  • Add the breadcrums, more grated cheese, and more parmesan on top
  • Grill for 10 minutes

This is a Mary Berry recipe.
